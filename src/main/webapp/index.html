About Us!

Snamh Swim is an Irish owned company with over 5 years’ experience operating local authority leisure centres throughout Ireland.

We are passionate about our customers achieving their goals, our communities, our people, the standards in our centres and the health & well-being of Ireland.

At the heart of our business is an ambitious vision of what we want to create. A Healthier and Happier Ireland.

What we offer

Beginner

Our Swim Instructors work closely with each child focusing on water awareness, confidence & safety to assist a smoother transition into the higher levels of the Snamh Swim!

Intermediate

Our goal is not only to teach your children to swim, but to help them develop an understanding of the water!

Advanced

Coaching is available for those who really enjoy swimming and are looking to take it to a competitive level.

Over 60's

There are various adult lessons available, for adults of all levels and ability. Our adult classes are structured into Non-Swimmer, Beginner, Intermediate and Advanced levels!
